About

About ScholarZim

ScholarZim is Zimbabwe's dedicated scholarship platform — built to connect students from Primary Grade 1 through Form 6 and tertiary study with verified funding opportunities.

We give every learner one place to search, compare, and apply, while scholarship providers publish programmes, review applications, and manage their pipeline with confidence.

ScholarFit matching helps students see where they qualify before they apply, so time is spent on opportunities that fit their level, field, and goals.

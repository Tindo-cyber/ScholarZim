About ScholarZim

Built for Zimbabwean students and funders

ScholarZim connects learners from primary school through postgraduate study with verified scholarships, bursaries, and funding opportunities across Zimbabwe and abroad.

Students build a clear academic profile, get ScholarFit matches, and track applications in one place. Providers publish listings, review applicants, and reach students who actually qualify.

  • Free for students to search and apply
  • Verified provider organisations
  • ZIMSEC-style exam results, not GPA-first
  • Mobile-friendly from Form school to university
